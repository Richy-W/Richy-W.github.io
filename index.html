Who Am I?

Before the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic, Richard began learning to code as a hobby. Although the process was long and arduous, it quickly became a passion. Once the pandemic hit in full force, Richard was laid off. Although this was a scary time for everyone, Richard decided to take this time build his skills and take his coding journey more seriously and enrolled in classes at Oklahoma City Community College. After three months of unemployment Richard returned to work, but this time with a renewed sense of purpose.
